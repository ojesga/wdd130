Nigeria

Nigeria is a vibrant country located in West Africa on the Gulf of Guinea. Known as the "Giant of Africa" due to its large population and economy, it features a diverse geography ranging from tropical rain forests to arid savannas. It is rich in cultural heritage, with over 250 ethnic groups, and is a major hub for fashion, cinema (Nollywood), and music across the continent.
