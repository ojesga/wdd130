Whitewater Adventures

Our mission is simple: to connect thrill-seekers and nature lovers alike with the raw power and beauty of the river. We operate with a strict creed of safety first, followed closely by unforgettable excitement. Whether you are looking for a relaxing family float or a heart-pounding Class V rapid run, we have a paddle waiting for you.

History

Founded in 2010 by a small group of passionate river guides, Whitewater Adventures began with just two rafts and a single van. Over the last decade and a half, we have grown into one of the premier rafting outfits in the region. Despite our growth, we remain true to our roots, treating every guest like family and protecting the beautiful waterways we call home.
